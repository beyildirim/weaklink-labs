For the SSDF attestation form, reference the CISA self-attestation
requirements (OMB M-22-18 and M-23-16). The key sections are:

- **2a (Secure environments):** Can you demonstrate build environment
  isolation, MFA, encryption, logging?
- **2b (Source code supply chain):** Do you scan for vulnerabilities
  before release?
- **2c (Provenance):** Do you track where your code and dependencies
  come from?
- **2d (Automated scanning):** Are vulnerability scans automated and
  ongoing?
- **2e (SBOM):** Do you generate and maintain SBOMs?
- **2f (Vulnerability disclosure):** Do you have a public disclosure
  mechanism?
- **2g (SSDF compliance):** Can you attest to overall SSDF conformance?

Be specific in the "Evidence" column. Do not write "yes" without citing
the specific tool, process, or artifact that supports the claim.

For the compliance roadmap, start with the gaps from your mapping
worksheet. Prioritize items that are required for federal sales (2a-2f)
over nice-to-haves.
